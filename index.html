Process & Methodology

Different projects can be approached in different ways, however below is my ideal design process.

Discovery

Collaborate with researchers to identify areas of improvement and understand user needs.

Workshops

Conduct/Participate in strategy & ideation sessions.


Design

Generate wireframes and/or realistic prototypes.


Test

The prototype gets tested to validate the ideas.


Build

Collaborate with Devs to build the solution.


Delivery & Monitoring

Deploy the solution and monitor the results.
