Process & Methodology

Different projects can be approached in different ways, however below is my general design process.

Explore

Search for innovation territories and understand user needs & requirements.

Ideas & Hypothesis

Gather ideas and hypothesis.


Design

Generate low-fidelity wireframes and/or realistic prototypes.


Test

The prototype gets tested to validate hypothesis.


Build

Collaborate with Devs to build the feature, or MVP.


Deliver

Optimize the solution and deploy.
