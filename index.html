Process & Methodology

A BAU job can have a slightly different process compared to a brand new feature or project, however below is my general way to approach a task.

Explore

Search for innovation territories and understand user needs & requirements.

Ideas & Hypothesis

Gather ideas and hypothesis.


Support Hypothesis

Gather facts through research to support the hypothesis rather than base them on pure assumptions.


Design

Generate low-fidelity wireframes and/or realistic mockups.


Test

The prototype gets tested to gather feedbacks and find areas for optimization.


Build

Collaborate with Devs to build the feature, or MVP.


Product Development

Contribution in developing features and internal tools. Wireframes in Figma/Sketch, user journey, planning & prioritization, user testing, documentation.
