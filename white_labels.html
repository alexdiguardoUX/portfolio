Multi-Brand Car Rental Platform for Affiliates

A new framework which different brands can adopt to offer an online car rental booking system

Project Overview

Context: One of the travel brands I worked for offers an affiliation program that allows affiliated companies to provide their customers with a car rental booking system under their own brand.

When: Q1 2021

My responsibilities:

  • Research
  • UI Design
  • Prototyping
  • Usability Testing

Business goal: Increase affiliates retention and attract new businesses to join our affiliate program.

Challenge prompt: The design of the current affiliate system is outdated and some features offered across brands are inconsistent. Design a new themable framework which all affiliated brands will sit on with their own look and feel.

The Process

User Research

Foundational user research was conducted with some of our biggest B2B affiliate representatives to understand their needs, goals and struggles.

The key question was: "What is important for your brand to have on a car rental platform?".

Key learnings:

  • All affiliates would like their brand to be well represented and consistent on all platforms
  • Most affiliates would like to display their own header & footer
  • Some affiliates would like to display promotional material
  • Some affiliates think it'd be good if they could have some control on the customisations

Who are we dealing with?

Our B2B persona was identified as a Brand Ambassador who represents one of our biggest affiliates.

Structure of the new design

Having a design system allows you to quickly generate medium/high fidelity mockups. Below is the design of the landing page with some annotations explaining how this is gonna fit with multiple brands.

Annotations:

  1. Custom Header: The affiliate can provide their own header to reinforce their brand presence and advertise other services they might offer
  2. Hero Section: Unnecessary distractions in the background of the image are removed so that it gives more legibility to the content. A tinted overlay will be applied to restore the natural color of the sky
  3. Promotional Content: When needed, the banner component can be enabled to display promotions. The image can be provided by the affiliate following our dimensions guidelines
  4. Custom Footer: Similar to the custom header, this will feature useful links

User Flow

An alternative path (in dark blue) has been introduced in the existing user journey to get to the search results. This represents a brand new page dedicated to special offers reachable when clicking on the banner.

The assumption here is that having special offers further down the landing page decreases visibility. This will later be validated during the A/B testing phase.

Usability Testing

20 participants were invited to a moderated remote testing phase to establish if the designs rightly represented some of our biggest affiliated brands and to identify areas of improvement.

Key findings:

  • Most participants recognised the affiliates' brand without showing the logo
  • Few participants were confused as they didn't know some brands also offered car rentals
  • Some participants weren't sure how to select the dates of the rental from the "Special Offers" page
  • All participants think the design is clean and appealing

Next Steps

  • Implement improvements based on the findings
  • Publish new components on our Design System
  • Handover to developers
  • A/B Test with few affiliates to track metrics like drop-outs, click-through and conversion
  • If successful, gradually rollout the new design to the affiliates, otherwise ri-iterate the design process
  • Explore solutions which allow affiliates to control some of the customisations
