In a nutshell...

The narrative: We live in an era where business opportunities are almost endless. For a car rental company with hundreds of affiliates, offering white-labelled products to airlines, hotels, and other businesses in the travel industry can turn into a gold mine.

Business goal: Increase affiliates retention and attract new businesses to join our affiliate program by modernising outdated solutions.

Success metrics: The solution attracted big brands like Jet2 and Ryanair which generated a total of 370k bookings between 2019-2020 (pre-covid).

Usability Testing

What do our end users think of it?

10 participants were invited to a moderated remote testing phase to establish if the designs rightly represented some of our biggest affiliated brands and to identify usability improvements. A fellow UX Researcher with access to Dscout helped with the recruitment.

Tasks:

  • Recognise a brand from the look & feel of the page, without displaying the logo
  • Being on a budget, book the most convenient car available in Manchester, UK

Key findings:

  • Most participants recognised the affiliates' brand without showing the logo
  • Most participants completed the tasks with minor issues
  • Few participants were confused as they didn't know some brands also offered car rentals
  • Some participants said they expect to see a "strikethrough" price if a special offer is applied
  • All participants think the design is clean and appealing

Implementation

How do we implement and manage a multitude of brands?

Thanks to the 'Swap Library' feature in Figma, it's now possible to create a brand file for each affiliate which will override the default theme with a single click.
